Redesigning Our School Playground

A modern, inclusive, and sustainable space for students

The Problem

The current playground is outdated and does not fully meet the needs of all students. It lacks green spaces, modern equipment, and areas for relaxation and social interaction.

Current Playground (Before)

Photo of the existing playground

Proposed Redesign (After)

AI-generated visualization of the redesigned playground

Design Improvements

  • If we add more green areas, students will feel more relaxed.
  • If we include sports and social spaces, students will be more active.
  • If we design inclusive areas, everyone will be able to enjoy the playground.
  • If we use modern materials, the playground will be safer.

Use of Artificial Intelligence

Artificial intelligence was used to generate a visual representation of the proposed playground redesign. The video is a conceptual visualization and not a final architectural plan.
